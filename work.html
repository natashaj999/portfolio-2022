  • About

    I’m a conscientious designer who uses empathy to connect with others and solve problems. I believe that staying curious and keeping an open-mind is integral to coming up with new ideas, and that inspiration can strike in the most unexpected places. While my dreams of travel are currently put on pause, I still find ways to explore through learning new programs and like to keep things fresh by trying new recipes, journaling, painting and changing up my routine every now and then! One of the most satisyfing parts about being a designer is taking the chaos and creating something out of it, and staying organized makes that process so much easier. Some of my earliest memories were noticing the art on album covers and dragging around my aisle and paints; I was driven from a young age, and the creative path feels like a natural fit.
